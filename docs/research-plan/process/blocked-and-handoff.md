# Blocked And Handoff Process



Use a blocked or handoff record when the research-plan pass cannot finish cleanly.



Record:



- status: blocked, failure, abnormal\_exit, or external\_stop

- current request

- evidence inspected

- what is missing or contradictory

- why continuing is not useful

- recommended next action

- suggested next orbit or human decision



Do not silently continue into implementation when the research or planning

contract is not satisfied.
